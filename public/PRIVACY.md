# Privacy Policy for Genrescope

**Last Updated**: January 8, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Genrescope. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

When you connect your Spotify account to Genrescope, we access the following data from Spotify:

- **Profile Information**: Your Spotify display name, email address, account type (Free/Premium), and profile picture
- **Listening History**: Your top artists and tracks based on your listening patterns
- **Genre Data**: Analysis of genres from your top artists

We do NOT collect:
- Your full listening history
- Your playlists
- Your saved songs or albums
- Any payment or billing information

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your Spotify data is used solely for the following purposes:

### Display Your Music Profile
We display your Spotify profile information and listening statistics on your Music DNA page.

### Generate Genre Analysis
We analyze your top artists' genres to create a genre breakdown showing your music taste distribution.

### Create AI Personality Description
We send your top tracks (song names and artists only) to OpenAI GPT-3.5 to generate a humorous, entertainment-focused personality description based on your music taste.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Spotify Web API
We connect to Spotify's API to retrieve your music data. This connection is governed by [Spotify's Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/privacy).

### OpenAI GPT-3.5
We send a formatted list of your top tracks to OpenAI's GPT-3.5 API to generate your personality description. This data is processed in accordance with [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

**Important**: The track data sent to OpenAI does NOT include your personal information (name, email, etc.), only song titles and artist names.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

### Session-Based Storage Only
- Your Spotify authentication tokens are stored in secure HTTP-only cookies during your session
- We do NOT store your Spotify data in any database
- We do NOT retain your music data after you disconnect or log out

### Temporary Processing
- Music data is retrieved only when you visit the Music DNA page
- GPT personality descriptions are generated on-demand and not permanently stored
- All data is automatically cleared when you disconnect your account

---

## 6. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

### View Your Data
You can see exactly what data we display at any time by visiting your Music DNA page.

### Disconnect Your Account
You can disconnect your Spotify account at any time by:
1. Going to Account Settings
2. Clicking "Disconnect Spotify Account"
3. This will immediately delete all your data and log you out

### Revoke Access
You can also revoke Genrescope's access directly in your [Spotify Account Settings](https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/).

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **HTTPS Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- **Secure Cookies**: Authentication tokens are stored in HTTP-only, secure cookies
- **OAuth 2.0 with PKCE**: We use Spotify's recommended OAuth 2.0 flow with PKCE for secure authentication
- **No Password Storage**: We never ask for or store your Spotify password
- **No Long-Term Data Storage**: Your data is never permanently stored on our servers
